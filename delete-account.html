Delete Your Account

Request deletion of your MCC East Bay app account and data

We respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of your MCC East Bay app account and what data will be affected.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose access to your saved events, giving history, prayer notification settings, and any other personalized data.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the MCC East Bay app
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted
  5. Your account will be deleted immediately

Option 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, send us an email with your account deletion request:

Please include the email address associated with your account.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Data Type Status
Profile information (name, email, phone) Deleted
Saved/bookmarked events Deleted
Ramadan giving goals and streaks Deleted
Prayer notification preferences Deleted
Event RSVPs and registrations Deleted
Push notification tokens Deleted
Donation records (if any)* Retained for legal compliance

*Note: Donation records processed through Kindful may be retained for tax and legal compliance purposes as required by law. Contact Kindful directly for questions about donation data.

Processing Time

  • In-app deletion: Immediate
  • Email requests: Within 7 business days

You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

After Deletion

  • You will be signed out of the app automatically
  • You can continue using the app as a guest (view prayer times, browse events)
  • You can create a new account at any time with the same or different email
